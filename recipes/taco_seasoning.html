Homemade Taco Seasoning

Description

DIY taco seasoning can be used in Mexican or Tex-Mex inspired dishes. Recipe from Karissa's Vegan Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Store in a jar for up to 1 year.

Note:Use about 2 tablespoons when a recipe calls for a packet of taco seasoning.
