Vegan Sugar Cookies

Description

Easy, 1-bowl, cut out vegan sugar cookie recipe from Karissa's Vegan Kitchen

No chilling required!

Ingredients

Sugar Cookies

  • 1 cup vegan butter softened (226g)
  • 1 cup sugar (200g)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour (360g)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (24g)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda

Icing

  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 3-6 tablespoons vegan milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • food coloring, sprinkles, etc for decorating

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together vegan butter, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl using a mixer or wooden spoon.
  3. Mix in the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and baking soda. At first, the dough will present as a sandy texture but will come together to form a cohesive dough. You may need to use your hands to squish the dough together.
  4. Form the dough into a ball. Roll it out to be about ¼-inch thick. I like to roll out my dough between two sheets of parchment paper to prevent the dough from sticking to the counter.
  5. Cut out the dough using cookie cutters.
  6. Transfer the cookie dough cut outs to the baking sheet. Bake for 8 minutes.
  7. Sometimes bubbles will form on top of the cookies. When fresh out of the oven, gently smooth out the top of the cookies with a spatula to create a flat surface.
  8. Let cool completely before frosting.

Icing

  1. Using a mixer or a whisk, combine the powdered sugar, 3 tablespoons of the milk, and the vanilla. Add more milk if needed to get the consistency you need.
  2. Add food coloring if desired.

Notes

If the dough is too crumbly and not forming into a ball even when using your hands, add a teaspoon of vegan milk. The dough should not be sticky at all.
